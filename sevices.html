Turtle Rescue

Services

Our purpose at Turtle Rescue is to protect and conserve turtles and their habitats. We achieve this goal by rescuing and rehabilitating wounded or sick turtles, conducting turtle behavior and ecology research, and educating the public about the importance of turtle conservation. Our professional team works tirelessly to ensure that every turtle in our care receives the best treatment and care possible. You can help us continue to provide these critical services and make a genuine impact in the fight to conserve these wonderful creatures by donating to our organization.
