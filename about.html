Turtle Rescue

About Us

Turtle Rescue is a non-profit organization dedicated to the protection and preservation of turtles and their habitats. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release injured and endangered turtles back into the wild. We believe that every turtle deserves a chance at a healthy and fulfilling life, and we work tirelessly to provide the necessary care and resources to make that a reality. Our team of dedicated volunteers is passionate about turtle conservation and committed to educating the public on the importance of conservation efforts. We believe that by working together, we can make a significant impact in the fight to protect these incredible creatures for future generations to enjoy. Thank you for your support of Turtle Rescue!
